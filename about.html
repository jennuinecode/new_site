About Me

OH HAYYY THERE! I'M JENN & AND I'M SO GLAD YOU'RE HERE.


First off, let me start off by saying that I HATE writing these things. I find it difficult to describe myself without sounding like I'm interviewing to be, like, someone's friend or something. Plus, who the hell wants to sit and read what I think about myself? I would much rather grab coffee with you, take you to my favorite donut place ever, and just let you figure it out.

Unfortunately, chances are that we have yet to meet in person, so a traditional 'about me' will have to do the job for now. (I'll try and make it quick and painless, I promise)

I'm a 25 year old Colombian-American creative soul with an organized mess of a brain. I am obssessed with donuts (but am ridiculously picky about them) and being the Colombian that I am, have a relationship with coffee that few would understand. I've been a portrait photographer for 6 years, a fur momma to my Wheaten Terrier (Kismet McGonagall Rodriguez) for 1.5 years, and stepped into the world of coding early last year.

I am also quite the fitness enthusiast, though last year I was living that #fatnessnotfitness life. I devoted myself to learning to code. Also, there were a few vacations where I had a little too much fun with the food last year, making fitness my like, #9 priority. I competed twice as an NPC bikini athlete, and though that is not in the forseeable future for me right now, would love to do it all over again. Right now, I am getting my mind and body back on that fitness track, which I will document via le bloggy blog.

I love Harry Potter, puppies, reading, sleeping in, wearing sport bras (what's a real bra?), coding, taking pictures people, and looking at pictures of food on Instagram...unfortunately, especially when I'm dying and can't eat said foods.

I am currently living in good ole Frisco, Texas with my amazing boyfriend, Ani and our Wheaten Terrier, Kismet. I have a full, beautiful life and I am so excited to be sharing it with you all. I hope that you enjoy my little space of the web as much as I do.

XO, Jenn
